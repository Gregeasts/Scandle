HOW TO PLAY

Scandle is a wordle variant where each row has a different rule. Rules are identified on the right of each row and are shown below.

Normal

A normal Wordle Puzzle row

Lie

One letter in a lie row is not showing its true colour!

Equal

The letters within the highlighted boxes must be identical.

End

The word must begin with the last letter of the previous word!

Block

Boxes covered black in this row will not reveal their shade.

Letters

The word must contain the letter in the highlighted box!
